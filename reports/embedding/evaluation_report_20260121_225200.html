ORKG Scoring System

The ORKG accuracy score is a position-weighted metric that rewards correct predictions appearing higher in the ranked list:

  • Rank 1 (Top-1 Match): 100% score - The correct field is the top prediction
  • Rank 2 or 3: 80% score - Correct field appears in positions 2-3
  • Rank 4 or 5: 60% score - Correct field appears in positions 4-5
  • Not in Top-5: 0% score - Correct field not found in top 5 predictions

Final score = Average of all paper scores

Hierarchical Distance (H-Distance)

Measures how far apart two fields are in the ORKG taxonomy tree:

  • 0 = Exact match - Same field in taxonomy
  • 1-2 = Close - Sibling or near-sibling fields (same parent)
  • 3+ = Distant - Different branches of taxonomy tree
  • N/A = Field not found in taxonomy

Note: H-Distance is for visualization only and does not affect accuracy scores.

Match Types

  • Top-1 Match: The first prediction exactly matches the ground truth
  • Top-5 Match: The ground truth appears somewhere in the top 5 predictions
